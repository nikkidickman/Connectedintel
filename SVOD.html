SVOD DASHBOARD

For business' offering of streaming video on-demand (SVOD) services. It is important to see the full picture of  across major streaming platforms such as Roku, iOS, and the web. Connected Intelligence provides a tool that can piece apart their volumes of data to understand their subscribers: who they are, why and when the make a purchase and why they stop subscriptions.

Features

1

Initiation Rates

Know how many prospects sign up for a trial subscription relative to the current date.

2

Paid Subscriptions

Visual metrics of owned and operated paid subs relative to the calendar date exactly one year prior and YOY varience.
# paid subs, start
# new conversions
conversion rate
# cancellations
cancellation rate
# paid subs, end
# net growth
% net growth

3

Retetion & Survival

Visualizaiton detailing retention. This shows, for all the subscriotions in a given month, how many subscriptions remain on 1 month, 2 months, and so on up to 12 months. You can also see a curve of the survival rates over 12 months for both LV and HMC products.

4

Cancellers

Insightful view of average monthly churn. This visual tells, on average, how many people unsubscribe from a campaign/product every month. You can also view a chart detailing the percent of at risk subs that churned over the past 12 months.

5

Platform

The Connected Intelligence platform gives you the ability to view the number of subscribers, cancellers and trials by platform (for example, X subscribers are from Roku, Y subscribers are from iOS, etc.). This includes the real value for each platform, and this value as a percentage of all subscribers. You can filter this chart based on product or campaign.

6

Country

You can view the number of subscribers by country (for example, X subscribers are from the USA, Y subscribers are from Canada, etc.). There are three countries: United States, Canada, and Other (the total of all other countries). This includes the real value for each country, and this value as a percentage of all subscribers. You can filter this chart based on product or campaign.

7

Engagement

Know the  number of subscribers by engagement (for example, X subscribers are dormant, Y subscribers are from occasional viewers, etc.). This includes the real value, the value as a percentage of all subscribers, total views, total minutes of viewing, average views per view, and average minutes per view, for each engagement level. You can filter this chart based on product or campaign.

8

Tenure

With your cusotmized dashboard you can view the number of subscribers by retention length. This includes the real value for each retention total, along with the total and as a percentage of all subscribers. You can filter this chart based on product or campaign.
